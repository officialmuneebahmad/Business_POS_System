## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our Point of Sale system seriously. If you find a security vulnerability, we appreciate it if you report it to us privately to protect active shop operations.

### How to Report
Please report security vulnerabilities by:
* Sending a direct email to: **officialmuneebahmed@gmail.com** (or your preferred security contact email)
* Or opening a private contact ticket through our official website: **[www.multantech.store](https://www.multantech.store)**

Please include:
1. A detailed description of the vulnerability.
2. Step-by-step instructions (or a proof-of-concept script) to reproduce the issue.
3. The potential impact of the exploit (e.g., pricing tampering, unauthorized database access).

### What to Expect
* **Initial Response:** You can expect an initial response acknowledging your report within **24 to 48 hours**.
* **Updates:** We will provide you with updates at least once every **3 days** while we investigate and work on a patch.
* **Resolution:** If the vulnerability is accepted, we will coordinate a patch release. Once the patch is deployed to production, we will publish a security advisory acknowledging your contribution (if desired).
